About Us

We are 4 programming enthusiasts who designed this game on our own time. We learned web design mostly on our own. We believe that education should be open and fun. This project is a true testament of that.

Just like programming, we believe games, visualization, and making connections help young children learning languages. This games teaches children to make memorable connections with common latin roots. The associations should come naturally with images and etymology. There is no need to force it mechanically.
